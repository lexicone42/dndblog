# Dava Gate

The largest airship terminal in Sharn, Dava Gate serves as the city's primary hub for aerial transportation to destinations across Khorvaire and beyond. Its multiple platforms and terminals handle everything from commercial passenger vessels to private charters.

## Layout

The terminal is organized into numbered terminuses, each capable of docking multiple airships:

- **Terminus 1-2**: Commercial passenger routes to major Khorvairian cities
- **Terminus 3**: Charter and private vessels (where the Stormlight was docked)
- **Terminus 4-5**: Cargo and freight operations
- **Terminus 6**: Lyrandar House exclusive berths
