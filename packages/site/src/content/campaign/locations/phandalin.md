# Phandalin

A small mining town and the party's original home. Here they heard the legend of the Bell of Shavras from a traveling storyteller, setting them on the path to adventure.

## Significance

- Starting location for the campaign
- Greeve was originally transformed into a Slaad here
- Party departed seeking the legendary Bell
