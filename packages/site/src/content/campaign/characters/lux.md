# Lux

A halfling woman with silver-streaked hair and eyes that see too much. Lux runs **The Floating Trinket**, a shop in Sharn's Sky Market that appears wherever it needs to be.

## Appearance

Short even for a halfling, Lux carries herself with the quiet confidence of someone who has seen much and judged little. Her silver-streaked dark hair frames a face lined with laugh lines and old sorrows.

## The Gift

When the party arrived at the Sky Market, Lux found them before they found her. She presented them with **The Heart of the Wild**, a legendary Dragonshard that the Emerald Enclave had been holding for whoever would next stabilize the ley anchors.

*"I've been holding this for someone. Turns out that someone is you."*

## Role

Lux serves as one of the Emerald Enclave's agents in Sharn's upper districts, using her shop's unique nature to pass messages and objects between those who need them.
