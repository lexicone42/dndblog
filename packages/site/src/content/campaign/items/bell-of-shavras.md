# Bell of Shavras

A legendary bell that started the party's adventure. According to the tale heard in Phandalin, the villagers of Shavras melted their gold into a bell and covered it with copper to hide its value from barbarian raiders.

## Composition

- 5,000gp worth of gold at the core
- 1,000cp worth of copper coating
- Ancient craftsmanship

## Significance

The legend of the Bell drew the party from Phandalin to the Shrine of Shavras, where they encountered the Fallen Priest and began their true adventure.
