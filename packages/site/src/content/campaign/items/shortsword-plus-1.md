# Shortsword +1

A finely honed blade that gleams with a faint magical aura. The weapon grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.

## Properties

**Magic Weapon.** You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

**Light & Finesse.** This weapon is ideal for dual-wielding and can use either Strength or Dexterity for attack and damage rolls.

## Significance

Kei wields this shortsword in their off-hand, complementing their fighting style with the vex weapon mastery to harass enemies in combat.
