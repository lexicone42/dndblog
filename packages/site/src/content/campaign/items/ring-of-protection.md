# Ring of Protection

A magical ring discovered in the Shavras Vault before it was sealed by Greeve's sacrifice.

## Properties

This ring offers magical protection to its wearer, granting bonuses to armor class and saving throws.

## History

### Discovery
Found among the treasures in the Shavras Vault during the party's exploration, alongside the Battering Shield, Bag of Holding, and Pipes of Haunting.

### Current Status
The ring was claimed before Greeve sealed the vault. Its current bearer is unknown.
