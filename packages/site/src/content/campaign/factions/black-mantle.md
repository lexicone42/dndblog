# The Black Mantle

A once-feared espionage syndicate that operated in Sharn's underworlds. Now apparently defunct or displaced.

## The Cover

The party traveled to Sharn under Black Mantle identities, provided by their Emerald Enclave contacts. The cover was meant to allow them to move freely through the city's darker corners.
