# The Party

The adventuring party at the heart of this chronicle. Originally from Phandalin, they followed the legend of the Bell of Shavras and became entangled in a cosmic struggle for the world's survival. Now in Xen'Drik, they continue their mission to stop the Slanted Circle.

## Active Members

- **Accoa Krigsdottir** - Paladin, Oath of the Ancients
- **Malum Munus Exemplar** - Assassin, shadow operative
- **Rudiger** - Wizard, evoker and arcane specialist
- **Skid Demon** - Ranger, ley-touched Stone-born
- **Stol** - Cleric, Life Domain healer

## Former Members

- **Kei Eaglesnout** - Fighter, retired in Session 11 to found the [Eaglesnout Academy](/eaglesnout-academy)
- **Greeve** - Now eternal guardian of the Shavras vault
- **Dain** - Betrayer, fate unknown

## Mission

Stabilize the ley anchors across multiple planes to prevent the awakening of Aru'xat the Verdant Maw. Currently investigating Circle activity in Xen'Drik.
