# Dhakaani Empire

An ancient goblinoid civilization that once ruled beneath what is now Sharn. Though fallen millennia ago, their undead guardians still protect the Heart Stone.

## Philosophy

Their inscriptions reveal their creed:
- "Hold the line."
- "The war is eternal."
- "Guard the heart."

## Hierarchy

- **Hobgoblin Warriors and Generals** - The military backbone
- **War Scholars** - Became Wraith-Tacticians in death
- **Warlords** - Supreme commanders (General Varkuun was the last)
