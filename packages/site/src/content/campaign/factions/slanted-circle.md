# The Slanted Circle

A dangerous cult conspiracy seeking to awaken Aru'xat the Verdant Maw, a primordial entity of immense destructive power.

## Identification

Members can be identified by:
- Purple robes
- Purple scarves
- Slanted Circle markings/tattoos

## Known Members

### The Circle Mage
- **Role**: Primary field operative
- **Abilities**: Powerful ice magic
- **Status**: Still at large

### Harlix Darkleaf
- **Role**: Deep cover infiltrator
- **Target**: The Emerald Enclave (specifically The Knock)
- **Status**: Captured (revealed as traitor in Session 8)

### Veyra
- **Race**: Changeling
- **Role**: Spy
- **Status**: Deceased

## Goals

Their ultimate objective is to align the ley lines in a specific configuration that will awaken Aru'xat the Verdant Maw. Success would mean catastrophic destruction.

## Threat Assessment

The Slanted Circle represents the primary antagonist faction of the campaign. Their infiltration of the Emerald Enclave demonstrates sophisticated intelligence capabilities, and The Circle Mage remains a dangerous threat.
