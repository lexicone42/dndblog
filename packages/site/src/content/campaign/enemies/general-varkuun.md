# General Varkuun

The last warlord of the Dhakaani Empire, bound by duty to guard the Heart Stone anchor for eternity.

## Description

Encased in ley-crystal armor that pulsed with the same light as the anchor. Eyes burning blue with ancient power. Even in undeath, he maintained the bearing of a supreme commander.
