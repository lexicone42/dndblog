# Acid Splash

*Evocation cantrip*

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a strip of white cloth)
**Duration:** 8 hours

You create an acidic bubble at a point within range, where it explodes in a 5-foot-radius Sphere. Each creature in that Sphere must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 Acid damage. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target’s Hit Point maximum and current Hit Points increase by 5 for the duration.

**At Higher Levels.** Each target’s Hit Points increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 2.
