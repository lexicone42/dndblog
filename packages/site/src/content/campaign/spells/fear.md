*Level 3 illusion*

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (a white feather)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Each creature in a 30-foot Cone must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or drop whatever it is holding and have the Frightened condition for the duration. A Frightened creature takes the Dash action and moves away from you by the safest route on each of its turns unless there is nowhere to move.
