# Invisibility

*Level 2 illusion*

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a grasshopper’s hind leg)
**Duration:** 1 minute

A creature you touch has the Invisible condition until the spell ends. The spell ends early immediately after the target makes an attack roll, deals damage, or casts a spell. You touch a willing creature. Once on each of its turns until the spell ends, that creature can jump up to 30 feet by spending 10 feet of movement.

**At Higher Levels.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.
