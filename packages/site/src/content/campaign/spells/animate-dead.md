# Animate Dead

*Level 3 necromancy*

**Casting Time:** 1 minute
**Range:** 10 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a drop of blood, a piece of esh, and a pinch of bone dust)
**Duration:** Instantaneous



**At Higher Levels.** You animate or reassert control over two additional Undead crea- tures for each spell slot level above 3. Each of the creatures must come from a different corpse or pile of bones.
