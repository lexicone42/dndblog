# Campaign Characters Reference

## The Party

### Stol
- **Class**: Cleric
- **Notable Abilities**: Turn Undead, healing magic
- **Key Moments**:
  - Fell during battle with the Fallen Priest, restored by Rudiger
  - Turn Undead was crucial against Dhakaani Wraith-Tacticians

### Kei
- **Class**: Fighter/Martial
- **Notable Abilities**: Combat prowess, strength
- **Key Moments**:
  - Won arm-wrestling competition in Astgrove (400gp prize)
  - Experienced vision of the weight of command and failure in Dhakaani ruins
  - Helped finish thugs at the Cornerstone Tavern in Sharn

### Rudiger
- **Class**: Wizard
- **Notable Abilities**: Fireball, Lightning magic
- **Key Moments**:
  - Internal conflict noted by Rook Targrave (given Scroll of Lightning Resistance)
  - Fireball was particularly effective on the Lightning Rail
  - Secured the ley stone from Nythraxil's lair

### Skid Demon
- **Notable Features**:
  - Carries a Dryad's bow (given by Erinthin)
  - Now has a ley mark that glows faintly
  - Can sense nearby ley currents
- **Key Moments**:
  - Fell through a ley gate crack, saw "rivers of light leading to a heart of stone"
  - Recognized as "Stone-born" by dwarf Glun
  - Had prophetic visions of world destruction if ley anchors break
  - His heartbeat syncs with ley pulses

### Accoa
- **Race**: Centaur
- **Origin**: Whisperwood Forest, taken by the Night Hag as a child
- **Key Moments**:
  - Joined party after helping defeat the Night Hag
  - Blessed by Dryad Erinthin
  - Stunned the pickpocket runner in Sharn

## NPCs - Allies

### Greeve
- **Status**: DECEASED / Now eternal guardian of Shavras vault
- **Background**: Had royal blood (unbeknownst to him), connected to bloodline murals in Shavras
- **Was**: Transformed into a Slaad, then restored
- **Sacrifice**: Impaled by the Fallen Priest's bone sword, chose to become a wraith guardian
- **Final Words**: "My family never told me of their royal blood, but my death will only seal the vault until they track down all of my ancestors."

### Aidin
- **Role**: Rescued captive, potential cleric
- **Background**: Was being used by the Fallen Priest for portal ritual
- **Status**: Saved, weak but alive after vault events
- **Connection**: Close to Greeve (he kissed her before his final charge)

### Dryad Erinthin
- **Location**: Whisperwood Forest heart
- **Role**: Forest guardian, blessed the party
- **Gift**: Gave Skid the Dryad's bow
- **Connection**: Known to the Emerald Enclave (Harlix recognized the bow)

### Harlix Darkleaf
- **Affiliation**: The Knock, Emerald Enclave
- **Role**: Initial contact, brought party to witness the Maw Frame
- **Combat**: Devastating in battle alongside Rudiger
- **Status**: TRAITOR - revealed in Sharn as working with the Slanted Circle
- **Confession**: Revealed location of Eberron ley anchor in Dhakaani dungeons

### Rook Targrave
- **Affiliation**: High Council of the Emerald Enclave
- **Role**: Briefed party on Aru'xat, the Slanted Circle, and ley anchors
- **Personality**: Stern, blunt, perceptive
- **Connection**: Sensed Rudiger's internal conflict, gave him scroll

### Captain Tallow
- **Role**: Ship captain of the Siren's Song
- **Secret**: Is a druid of Eberron
- **Abilities**: Can banish creatures (banished the kraken), perform mist-parting rituals
- **Location**: Muck Bay

### Master Molrik
- **Affiliation**: Dwarven clan beneath Sharn
- **Role**: Led party to Dhakaani ruins, took custody of Harlix and Dragonshards
- **Clan**: Rewarded party with magical items and eternal respect

### Alkanar
- **Race**: Dragonborn
- **Role**: Elderly historian of the Emerald Enclave
- **Expertise**: Briefed party on Sharn and Eberron

### Elder Elarion Mossbark
- **Affiliation**: Verdant Hollow (Emerald Enclave chapter in Sharn)
- **Role**: Warned party about Harlix being tracked, revealed ley shard tracking

## NPCs - Enemies

### The Fallen Priest
- **Status**: DESTROYED (twice)
- **Base**: Shrine of Shavras
- **Powers**: Necromancy, portal magic, bone sword, became a wraith shade
- **Goal**: Opening portal to the Undergods
- **Final Form**: Dark shade that rose in the vault
- **Defeat**: Required Greeve's bloodline sacrifice to seal

### Night Hag
- **Status**: DEFEATED
- **Location**: Whisperwood Forest
- **Crime**: Kidnapped Accoa as a child, cursed the forest
- **Method**: Drained victims slowly in their sleep

### Veyra (Changeling)
- **Status**: DECEASED
- **True Role**: Slanted Circle agent posing as party's contact
- **Deception**: Posed as serving girl, then rail agent, fed all party movements to the Circle
- **Final Words**: "Did you truly think I was your ally? Every step, every whisperâ€”I fed to the Circle."

### Dain
- **Status**: BETRAYER (fate unknown)
- **Former Role**: Party companion
- **Betrayal**: Led party into ambush at Umbrage Hill, revealed as working with the Fallen Priest
- **Method**: Hidden door triggered trap/attack

### The Circle Mage
- **Status**: AT LARGE
- **Affiliation**: Slanted Circle
- **Encounters**:
  1. Shipwreck during kraken attack - escaped by diving into water
  2. Stonehell Isle - killed Nythraxil, fell down mountain (survived)
  3. Heart Stone Chamber - appeared when anchor core opened, then vanished
- **Abilities**: Ice magic, powerful enough to sever dragon wing
- **Description**: Sigil burned into tattered robes, eyes glowing with ley reflection

### Ulfandor
- **Status**: Unknown (likely hostile)
- **Description**: White dragon seen flying during Shavras battle
- **Mystery**: Unknown why dragon was present and left

### General Varkuun
- **Status**: DEFEATED
- **Identity**: Last Dhakaani warlord
- **Appearance**: Encased in ley-crystal armor, eyes burning blue
- **Final Words**: "You are the last commanders. Guard what we could not."

## Primordial Threat

### Aru'xat the Verdant Maw
- **Status**: SEALED (threatened)
- **Type**: Primordial serpentine entity, "Feeder of Forests"
- **Origin**: Sealed during the Dawn Wars when gods first planted life
- **Threat**: Once devoured entire worlds
- **Awakening Method**: Slanted Circle aligning ley lines via Maw Frame
- **Connection**: The Throat of Aru'xat / Maw Frame is a resonance engine to wake him

## Ancient Beings

### Nythraxil
- **Status**: DECEASED
- **Type**: Rose quartz dragon (ancient)
- **Location**: Stonehell Isle (banished there)
- **Physical State**: So ancient his body fused to stone
- **Contribution**: Provided ley stone (also fused to quartz)
- **Cause of Death**: Circle mage's ice blast severed his wing, died after breathing fire at attacker
- **Prophecy**: Directed party to seek the Jundites of Eberron and word of Ildrar, cleric of Orondin

### Ildrar
- **Status**: Unknown (sought)
- **Role**: Cleric of the ancient race of Orondin
- **Importance**: Holds "the word" needed for ley anchors
