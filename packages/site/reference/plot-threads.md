# Campaign Plot Threads Reference

## Main Threat: Aru'xat the Verdant Maw

### The Entity
- **Type**: Primordial serpentine being
- **Title**: "The Feeder of Forests"
- **History**: Sealed during the Dawn Wars when gods first planted life
- **Power Level**: Once devoured entire worlds
- **Status**: SEALED but threatened

### The Awakening Plan
- **Executor**: The Slanted Circle
- **Method**: Align ley lines using the Maw Frame resonance engine
- **Location**: North Woods of Astralglades
- **Progress**: Maw Frame under construction, enslaved workers building it

### If Released
- Skid's vision showed: Sharn's towers collapsing, oceans boiling, forests splitting along glowing fault lines
- Ley prophecy: "If the pulse breaks, the world will bleed. The roots that bind will tear, and the sky will burn."
