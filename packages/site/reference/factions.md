# Campaign Factions Reference

## The Emerald Enclave

### Overview
- **Type**: Druidic organization dedicated to protecting nature
- **Reach**: Multi-planar (chapters in multiple worlds including Eberron)
- **Symbol**: Leaf pendant worn on right breast

### Leadership
- **High Council**: Governs major decisions
  - **Rook Targrave**: Stern council member, briefed party on main threat

### The Knock
- **Type**: Subfaction of the Emerald Enclave
- **Role**: "Protectors of the wildlands, friends of the forest"
- **Base**: Astgrove region
- **Known Members**:
  - Harlix Darkleaf (TRAITOR - revealed in Session 8)
  - Rangers in forest green

### Verdant Hollow (Sharn Chapter)
- **Location**: Hidden in a hollowed tower garden in Sharn
- **Leader**: Elder Elarion Mossbark
- **Status**: Divided - internal suspicion about traitors

### Key Resources
- The Keyport (planar travel junction)
- Ley stones for powering planar travel
- Network of contacts across planes

### Current Goals
- Stop the Slanted Circle from awakening Aru'xat
- Anchor the ley lines to prevent world destruction
- Track down ley anchors across planes

## The Slanted Circle

### Overview
- **Type**: Cult/conspiracy seeking to awaken primordial entity
- **Goal**: Align ley lines to wake Aru'xat the Verdant Maw
- **Symbol**: Purple robes, purple scarves, Slanted Circle markings

### Known Members
- **The Circle Mage**: Primary field operative, powerful ice magic, still at large
- **Harlix Darkleaf**: Infiltrated Emerald Enclave, now captured
- **Veyra**: Changeling spy, deceased
- Various warriors and agents

### Methods
- Infiltration of opposing organizations
- Kidnapping and enslaving workers for construction
- Assassination (killed Yog the goblin informant)
- Using scrying crystals to track enemies

### Major Project: The Maw Frame
- **Location**: North Woods of Astralglades
- **Type**: "Throat of Aru'xat" - resonance engine
- **Purpose**: Awaken Aru'xat by aligning ley lines
- **Method**: Forced labor, cutting ancient forest
- **Status**: Under construction

### Allies/Resources
- Backed by a Dragonmarked House (unidentified)
- Access to Dragonshard shipments
- Druidic cipher books (stolen)

## The Black Mantle

### Overview
- **Type**: Shadowy espionage syndicate
- **Reputation**: Feared in Sharn's underworlds
- **Status**: Apparently defunct or displaced in Sharn
- **Symbol**: Black wax sigil of crossed swords

### Party's Use
- Party traveled to Sharn under Black Mantle identities
- Cover blown when thugs revealed "the Mantle don't walk Sharn no more"
- Identities were intentionally given to make party easier to track (per Elder Mossbark)

## The Dhakaani Empire

### Overview
- **Type**: Ancient goblinoid civilization
- **Status**: Fallen/ruins remain
- **Legacy**: Built Sharn's foundations, sealed ley anchor beneath

### Hierarchy (Historical)
- Hobgoblin warriors and generals
- War Scholars (became Wraith-Tacticians in death)
- Warlords (General Varkuun was the last)

### Philosophy
- **Inscriptions Found**:
  - "Hold the line."
  - "The war is eternal."
  - "Guard the heart."
- **Purpose**: Guarded the ley anchor / Heart Stone

### Current State
- Ruins beneath Sharn
- Undead guardians still protect the depths
- Statues "bowed" and released light motes when balance was restored

## Molrik Clan

### Overview
- **Type**: Dwarven clan
- **Location**: Beneath Sharn, near Dhakaani ruins
- **Leader**: Master Molrik

### Relationship with Party
- Sensed ley flux from party's arrival
- Recognized Skid as "Stone-born"
- Led party to Dhakaani ruins
- Took custody of Harlix and captured Dragonshards
- Rewarded party with magical items and eternal respect

### Member: Glun
- Recognized Skid's ley connection
- Called him "Stone-born"

## The Undergods

### Overview
- **Type**: Ancient malevolent deities
- **Status**: Sealed away
- **Portal**: The Fallen Priest attempted to open a portal to them

### Connection to Main Plot
- The vault of Shavras was sealed to prevent Undergods portal
- Fallen Priest served the Undergods
- May be connected to Aru'xat's imprisonment during Dawn Wars

## Historical: The Dawn Wars

### Overview
- **Type**: Ancient conflict
- **Participants**: The gods vs. primordial entities
- **Outcome**: Aru'xat sealed, life planted on world

### Significance
- Origin of Aru'xat's imprisonment
- Ancient beings from this era may help anchor ley lines
- Nythraxil was banished during or after this period
