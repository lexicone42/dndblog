# Campaign Items & Artifacts Reference

## Major Artifacts

### The Ley Stone (Stonehell)
- **Origin**: Nythraxil's lair, fused to quartz
- **Obtained**: Session 7, secured by Rudiger
- **Purpose**: Anchors ley lines in the Astralglades
- **Current Status**: Given to Rook and the Emerald Enclave
- **Limitation**: Only anchors Astralglades ley lines; other anchors needed elsewhere

### The Heart Stone / Eberron Ley Anchor
- **Location**: Heart Stone Chamber beneath Sharn
- **Description**: Black obsidian monolith veined with pulsing light
- **Function**: Ley anchor for Eberron, "heart" of the ley network
- **Status**: STABILIZED by party in Session 9
- **Connection**: Skid now linked to it via ley mark

### The Bell of Shavras (Trinket)
- **Origin**: Legend from Phandalin traveler
- **History**: Villagers melted gold into bell, covered with copper to hide value
- **Worth**: 5,000gp gold + 1,000cp copper coating
- **Location**: Found atop ruined shrine of Shavras

### The Maw Frame / Throat of Aru'xat
- **Type**: Massive wooden construction
- **Location**: North Woods of Astralglades
- **Purpose**: Resonance engine to awaken Aru'xat
- **Description**: Resembles jaw of giant creature
- **Status**: Under construction by enslaved workers
