# Campaign Locations Reference

## Starting Region

### Phandalin
- **Type**: Small mining town
- **Significance**: Starting location for the campaign
- **Events**:
  - Party originally from here
  - Heard legend of the Bell of Shavras from a traveler
  - Greeve originally transformed into Slaad here

### Umbrage Hill
- **Type**: Hill/ruins area
- **Significance**: Site of Dain's betrayal
- **Events**:
  - Party ambushed after Dain triggered hidden door
  - First encounter with forces of the Fallen Priest

---

## The Fallen Priest's Domain

### Shavras (Shrine)
- **Type**: Ancient ruined shrine
- **History**: Once a village repeatedly sacked by barbarians
- **The Bell**: Villagers melted gold into a bell covered with copper (5000gp + 1000cp)
- **Events**:
  - Final battle with the Fallen Priest (first form)
  - Portal ritual interrupted
  - White dragon Ulfandor sighted flying away

### Shavras Vault (Underground)
- **Type**: Ancient sealed dungeon
- **Access**: Opened after Priest's body liquified into the earth
- **Features**:
  - Shadow memories of ancient priests replaying on walls
  - Murals depicting sacred bloodlines (Greeve recognized his family crest)
  - Multiple chambers with guardians
- **Enemies Encountered**: Goblins, skeletal warriors, ogre, giant spiders
- **Heart Chamber**: Purple light, floating box containing a heart
- **Status**: SEALED - Greeve is now eternal guardian
- **Treasures Found**: Ring of Protection, Battering Shield, Bag of Holding, Pipes of Haunting

## Whisperwood Region

### Whisperwood Forest
- **Type**: Cursed forest
- **Curse**: Night Hag's influence, undead patrols
- **Features**: Dark, twisted trees, unnatural fog
- **Events**:
  - Party wandered for days
  - Encountered undead patrols
  - Eventually led to forest heart by following signs

### Forest Heart (Whisperwood)
- **Type**: Sacred grove
- **Guardian**: Dryad Erinthin
- **Events**:
  - Dryad blessed the party
  - Revealed Night Hag's location
  - Accoa recognized as former captive

### Night Hag's Lair
- **Type**: Hidden den in Whisperwood
- **Status**: CLEARED
- **Events**: Party defeated the Night Hag, freed Accoa

---

## Astgrove Region

### Astgrove
- **Type**: Large sub-alpine town
- **Population**: ~3,000
- **Features**:
  - Jeweler (party sold gems for 6000gp)
  - Arcane shop (Eyes of the Eagle, Goggles of Night)
  - Custom armor smith
  - Tavern: The Mason and the Chain
- **Significance**: Base for the Emerald Enclave / The Knock operations

### The Mason and the Chain
- **Type**: Tavern in Astgrove
- **Events**:
  - Party first met Harlix Darkleaf (shot man stumbled into them outside)
  - Kei won arm-wrestling tournament (400gp)

### The Astralglades / North Woods
- **Type**: Ancient forest north of Astgrove
- **Current Status**: CORRUPTED / ACTIVE THREAT
- **Features**:
  - Ley lines visible as glowing blue energy
  - Gravity anomalies (rocks rolling uphill)
  - Ancient trees being cut down
- **The Maw Frame**: Massive wooden jaw-like structure under construction
- **Enemies**: Purple-robed Slanted Circle members, enslaved humans

## Stonehell Isle

### Stonehell Isle
- **Type**: Hidden island
- **Protection**: Enchanted mist hides location
- **Access**: Captain Tallow's ritual parts the mist
- **Features**: Massive stone spires, giant vultures

### Nythraxil's Lair
- **Location**: Summit of Stonehell Isle
- **Entry Requirement**: Release memories into crystal pools
- **Description**: Ancient dragon's final resting place
- **Features**: Rose quartz formations fused with dragon's body
- **Status**: DESTROYED - Circle mage collapsed part of it
- **Important Item**: Ley stone obtained here

## Eberron (Different Plane)

### Sharn - The City of Towers
- **Type**: Massive vertical city
- **Structure**: Five plateaus, towers reaching skyward
- **Built Upon**: Ancient Dhakaani goblinoid ruins
- **Transportation**: Lightning Rail connects to distant lands
- **Underworld**: Black markets, espionage networks

### The Keyport
- **Type**: Ancient planar junction
- **Location**: Beneath the Heartwood Oak
- **Function**: Powered by ley stones, enables travel between planes
- **Note**: Party was blindfolded when led there

### Callestan District (Sharn)
- **Type**: Lower city district
- **Features**: Narrow streets, bazaars
- **Notable Location**: Cornerstone Tavern

### Cornerstone Tavern
- **Type**: Fighting pit tavern in Callestan
- **Features**: Mercenary brawls, gambling
- **Events**:
  - Party met changeling contact (actually Veyra)
  - Ambushed by thugs questioning their Black Mantle identity
  - Purple-scarved Slanted Circle agents spotted

### Verdant Hollow
- **Type**: Hidden Emerald Enclave chapter house
- **Location**: Hollowed tower garden in Sharn
- **Leader**: Elder Elarion Mossbark
- **Events**: Party warned about Harlix's suspicious behavior

### Tavick's Landing
- **Type**: District in Sharn
- **Features**: Lightning Rail terminal, cargo platforms
- **Events**:
  - Party intercepted Dragonshard shipment
  - Harlix and Veyra revealed as traitors
  - Lightning Rail battle

### Molrik's Forward Camp
- **Type**: Dwarven outpost beneath Sharn
- **Features**: Ancient dwarven forges lit by magma streams
- **Clan**: Molrik (Master Molrik leads)
- **Events**: Party given access to Dhakaani ruins

## Dhakaani Ruins (Beneath Sharn)

### The Dhakaani Empire Ruins
- **Type**: Ancient goblinoid civilization remains
- **Location**: Deep beneath Sharn
- **Ley Activity**: Strongest ley energy felt here
- **Note**: Skid's heartbeat synced with ley pulse

### Hall of Warriors
- **Features**: Statues of hobgoblin warriors with inscriptions
- **Inscriptions**: "Hold the line." "The war is eternal." "Guard the heart."
- **Phenomena**: Time echoes - hall came alive showing ghostly soldiers
- **Visions**: Each party member experienced different visions of the past war

### Hall of Seals
- **Type**: Trapped chamber
- **Hazard**: Rune-trapped tiles with force-field blades
- **Solution**: Decipher correct path

### War Scholars' Quarters
- **Enemies**: Three Dhakaani Wraith-Tacticians
- **Death Whisper**: "The heart below must never fall..."

### Gauntlet of Stone & Shadow
- **Enemies**: Six undead hobgoblin warriors, two Stonebound Guardians, Shadow Captain
- **Reward**: Command token pointing to final chamber

### Heart Stone Chamber
- **Type**: Cathedral-like chamber, final destination
- **The Anchor**: Black obsidian monolith veined with pulsing light
- **Features**: Twelve statues of hobgoblin generals, ley conduits into walls
- **Voice**: Spoke in skulls: "The Heart endures. Who commands the stone?"
- **Boss**: General Varkuun
- **Events**:
  - Defeated General Varkuun
  - Circle Mage appeared and vanished
  - Skid had prophetic vision
  - Anchor stabilized
- **Status**: SECURED - Ley anchor stable
